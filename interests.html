Interests

Outside of my classes and projects, here are a few things that pique my interest.

Speedcubing

I got into speedcubing when I was 13. My favorite puzzle is the 3x3 cube, but I also enjoy solving the 2x2, 4x4, 5x5, and pyraminx. My personal best time on the 3x3 is 12.06 seconds.

Biking

I've been riding bikes ever since I was a little kid. Nowadays, I enjoy going on long-distance bike rides, both through my hometown and on bike trails, such as the Mohawk-Hudson bike trail in Upstate New York. It's not only a great way to get some fresh air and exercise, but also serves as a stress reliever.

Traveling

I love traveling. I really enjoy seeing new places, learning about new cultures and history, and especially trying new cuisines. So far, I've visited 18 US states and 6 countries outside the US.
